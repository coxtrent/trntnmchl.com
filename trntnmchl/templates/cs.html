WCBN app

WCBN is the University of Michigan's student-run radio station. I developed an iOS app for them that allows users to stream the station live, view the schedule, and see the latest news. The app is written in Swift and uses the AVFoundation library for streaming audio. The app is available on the App Store.

WCBN sub board

WCBN is, again, the University of Michigan's student-run radio station. Occasionally students and community members are unable to attend their regularly scheduled programs and must ask other DJs to host their shows at the regular time.

The sub board was the station's solution to this; requests live on the website, can be posted with a valid DJ login, and mass emails are sent for new requests. However, the station's ever-changing backend has rendered its tech solutions useless many a time. Thus, I rebuilt this tool from scratch.
